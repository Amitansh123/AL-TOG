About AL-TOG

A learning platform for all the competitive programmers by helping them analyze their progress and learn collaboratively by managing their friends.A tool to analyse and improve your Competitive Programming Progress.The goal is to keep people motivated in algorithmic programming and solve more and more problems. Find a friend who is similar to you and just compete among each other. This will make environment competitive and you'll definitely give your best. It will make things interesting. You will also upsolve when you got a nice company (/competitor). It's not like you are enemies, you will teach each other something.
