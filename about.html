About AL-TOG

A learning platform for all the competitive programmers by helping them learn and analyze their progress across all the platforms.A tool to analyse and improve your Competitive Programming Progress.The goal is to keep people motivated by helping them track their progress in data structure and algorithms and problem solving. This encourages users to solve more and more problems and increase the accuracy
